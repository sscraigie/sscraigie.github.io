Austin Craigie

Growing up I loved messing with computers, and that love grew when my older brother showed me Scratch. After playing with scratch for a while I knew that I wanted to learn programming and go to school to study it. Currently I am a student at Washington State University majoring in Computer Science. The first language that learned was C and C++, but I have also done some work with Ruby, HTML, CSS, C#, and ML (Meta Language). Besides school I have been involved in the WSU Robotics Club, serving as their 2017 Treasurer, started a club Game Jam, a hackathon participant and a Rotary Youth Leadership Award winner (RYLA).

As I have learned, made programs and projects there was no good place for me to display my work for people to see. So that is what this site is for. A lot of my favorite work is on this website and it has become one of my favorite projects.

Thank you for taking the time to look at my site and I hope you enjoy.
